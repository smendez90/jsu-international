Welcome Message from the International Programs Director

We at Jacksonville State University recognize and appreciate the fact that International students like yourself add tremendous value to the campus experience that each student at JSU can have. We are fortunate that we get to serve over 200 international students from Europe, Asia, North and South America, Africa, and the Middle East, are enrolled here.

Our goals remain intact which is to always strive to provide you with an environment in which you can easily learn and get exposure to American culture, and in which students from Alabama can learn about your culture. JSU has shown a long commitment to an international environment on campus with the establishment in 1946 of one of the first International House Programs in the country. Today, we are proud to continue this tradition.

We undoubtedly live in a very globally connected world where we have an absolute obligation to provide inter-cultural opportunity to all of our students including yourself. We therefore, seek to continue welcoming our international students from all around the world and give you an opportunity to engage with the entire campus while pursuing your academic goals.

As a former International student, I understand the need for students to feel accepted and cared for. We at Jacksonville State University, takes pride in our belief that we are here to serve our students. We serve each individual students with respect and care no matter where they come from. All of our students become part of one big Gamecock Family once they come to JSU. I look forward to seeing each one of you.


Chandni Khadka,
Director International Programs
Jacksonville State University

Housing

Housing Accommodations

Choosing the Office of Housing Operations and Residence Life means more than just putting a roof over your head. JSU strives to provide our students with pleasant, furnished facilities that include special features and extras. The JSU residential community is comprised of eight residence halls, seven apartment complexes, and three specialty houses, all within walking distance of the campus.

General Amenities for Residence Halls

All JSU residential facilities are air-conditioned, provide free utilities, and free laundry. Custodial service is provided to clean public areas only in the residence halls Monday-Friday. Security is provided by the University Police Department (UPD) during the night, and additional measures have been implemented at each building. Resident Assistants (RAs) work security during visitation hours, and Night Desk Assistants (NDAs) provide security after visitation hours. Card access systems at each front entrance provide additional security for our residents.
