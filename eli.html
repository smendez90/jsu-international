English Language Institute

The ELI is designed for students who want to improve their English because they are interested in studying at a college of university in the United States. The classes are small, allowing much individual attention from caring, well-trained instructors. Not only will students improve their English, but they will also learn about American cutlure in a safe, friendly environmnet.

Important Dates

Application Deadline: The ELI offers six sesions per year. Please apply six weeks before the starting date of the session for which you plan to begin your studies.
