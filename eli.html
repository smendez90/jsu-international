English Language Institute

The English Language Institute will prepare you for successful university study. When you study English at ELI you become part of the university—your JSU student ID card gives you access to housing, dining, health services, sports and entertainment events. At ELI, you will enjoy a college experience before you start college!

What I will study

In classes of 12 or fewer students, our experienced professors will enable you to use American English academically and socially with confidence. Here is a typical schedule:

In addition to 20 hours per week of core English skills courses, you can choose 5 more hours of elective courses. In addition, you can participate in 3 hours per week of "Introduction to JSU", where you will learn more about studying at an American university by visiting real classes, meeting other international students, and participating in university and community events and activities.

English Requirements

There are six levels in the program, but most students should start at Level 2 or 3 (you will take a placement test). Students taking the TOEFL or IELTS exams usually reach their required university entrance score during Level 4 or 5.

Conditional Admission: If you intend to study at JSU, you can declare "Conditional Admission" at the beginning of your studies. Then, after successful completion of ELI Level 4, you can be admitted to the University without a TOEFL or IELTS score.

Apply Now

Before you apply, make sure you have the following

  • Have an personal email address.
  • Have a valid passport, and a copy of the photo page
  • You will need $35 for an application fee
  • Select your sessions to prepare the amount that you are planning to study English and issue an 1-20
  • Calculate cost and make sure to include all possible expenses for your stay at the ELI program. You will be required to evidence of funding.
  • A bank statement it must be yours and sponsoring relative’s, it must show at total balance, and it must be recent.

When you are ready you will

  1. Apply online fill out application or ELI students. Be sure that your name of and date of birth are the same as on your passport.

  2. Email documents email your passport and bank statement documents to eli@jsu.edu
