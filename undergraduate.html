Undergraduate Programs

More than one million students from around the world choose to come to the United States for their higher education. Degrees earned at accredited institutions in the United States are recognized worldwide and prep5are you to be a global citizen.

We at Jacksonville State University recognize the need for our students to be global citizens to compete in the real world once they graduate. We therefore celebrate diversity and welcome students from all around the world.

Take a look at our accreditation

Accreditation

All of our academic programs are accredited. Some of our programs are accredited by worldwide accreditation bodies such as: Our Computer Science and Information System is recognized by Computing Accreditation commission of ABET Our school of business and industry is recognized by AACSB International For a list of all acreditations at JSU click here.

Acreditation Agencies

Jacksonville State University is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges to award bachelor’s, master’s, educational specialist, and doctoral degrees.

Education Curriculum

In the United States, students are required to complete a certain number of credit hours from the general education curriculum to broaden their knowledge in diverse areas such as English, Humanities and Fine Arts. Natural Sciences, Mathematics, History, Social and behavioral science.

Students will have the opportunity to choose from variety of general education courses, electives and major courses depending on the major they select.

Student Life

JSU is the friendliest campus in the South. Students are welcomed and accepted with Southern hospitality.

Students are able to enjoy in outdoor activities within JSU sport venues and the city of Jacksonville all year round since the weather in the region is not extreme.

International Student Organization (ISO) provides a platform for international students to be creative with organizing activities on campus. As an ambassador for one's unique culture, each ISO members hold responsibility of educating campus community and Calhoun county community of their respective culture.

Learn more about our student life on campus here

After Graduation

International Students under F-1 visa category can apply to work Under OPT (Optional Practical training) in the United States for 12 months after graduating from their degree program. Students in STEM field are eligible for 24 extra months to be employed in their field of study. JSU international alumni are employed by Fortune 100 companies.

Students at JSU are able to participate in many career fairs and professional preparedness for you to apply for the different job opportunities with help from our Career Services.

International Students may want to consider pursuing a Graduate degree program. Having an advanced degree will/may give you an opportunity to negotiate a better career option and higher financial reward.

English Requirements

In order to ensure your academic success at JSU, we ask for proof of English proficiency test score. You will have many options to choose from when it comes to taking the English proficiency test. Some of the popular ones are TOEFL, IELTS, PTE, iTEP etc.

Exemptions

Graduating from a high school (minimum 2 years of attendance and completion of 2 English courses) in a country where the official language is English.
